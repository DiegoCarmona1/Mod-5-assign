Services

The services we have available for our dear clients are provided to better care for their needs, to better their agricultural experiences, as well as better the relationship between us and the customer through support and familiarity. Some of these services include:

  1. Equipment maintenance
  2. Machine repair
  3. Inspection and violation detection
  4. Agricultural Animal care and check-up

All these services are provided at a cost, other than the call center that is available between the times of 10:00 am - 6:00 pm PST. For any questions, comments, concerns, or other inquiries, please refer to the 'Contact Us' page where the call center number will receive your call and attempt to assist you to the best of their abilities.
